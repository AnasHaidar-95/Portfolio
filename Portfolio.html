About Me

Creative Developer & Designer

I’m a fast-learning, highly motivated full-stack developer who turns ideas into polished digital experiences. What began as curiosity about how a button click makes things happen evolved into a passion for building complete web applications that are as elegant under the hood as they are on the surface. After an intensive 17-week boot-camp and 500+ hours of hands-on coding sprints, I earned certification as a Junior Full-Stack Developer and have been shipping production-ready apps ever since.

I specialize in JavaScript across the stack—React and vanilla JS for responsive, engaging interfaces, Node and Express for robust APIs, and MongoDB or SQL for reliable data layers. I’m comfortable translating user stories into clean, scalable code, and writing tests that keep regressions out of production. Whether working solo or in agile teams, I bring a detail-oriented mindset, clear communication, and a relentless focus on delivering features that solve real problems.

When I’m not coding, you’ll find me exploring new frameworks, contributing to open-source projects, or diving into the next technical challenge. I believe technology is at its best when it’s invisible to the user yet powerful enough to change how they work and live—let’s build something that makes that difference together.
